Travel HUB FAQ

Booking & Payment

How can I book a flight?
You can book directly through our TravelHUB website or app.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and travel vouchers.
Can I use promo codes?
Yes! Apply them at checkout for discounts.
Can I pay later?
We offer 'book now, pay later' for selected flights.
Is there a booking fee?
Some bookings may include a minimal processing fee.
Can I get an invoice?
Yes, invoices are sent automatically after payment.
What if my payment fails?
Retry or contact support to resolve payment issues.
Is my payment information secure?
Absolutely. We use encrypted payment gateways for security.

Flights

How do I change my flight?
Login to your account and modify your booking details.
Can I cancel my booking?
Yes, depending on your ticket’s cancellation policy.
What if my flight is delayed?
You’ll receive alerts and support for rebooking.
How early should I check in?
Check in at least 2 hours before domestic flights.
Can I select my seat?
Yes! Seat selection is available during booking.
Will I get notified about flight changes?
Yes, via email or push notifications from our app.
Are meals provided on board?
Depends on the airline and ticket type.
Can I add extra services?
Yes, you can add baggage, meals, and insurance while booking.
